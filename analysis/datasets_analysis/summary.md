# Dataset Characterization for BioNLP @ ACL 2026 Paper

*Generated: 2026-04-10 by `run_analysis.py`*

Compares Phenopacket (all and 6,901 phenodp-compatible subset), RAMEDIS, LIRICAL, HMS, and MME against the complete HPO disease ontology (phenotype.hpoa, hp-version 2025-09-01).

## Reference ontology

- phenotype.hpoa contains **12,717** rare diseases with phenotype (P aspect) annotations (8,356 OMIM, 4,314 ORPHANET, 47 DECIPHER).
- **11,323** distinct HPO terms are used across those annotations.
